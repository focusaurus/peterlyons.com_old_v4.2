Oh my God, you guys, Susan Oetgen's second Likeness to Lily album is almost out! Susan is a beautiful singer that I met through my brother. She's trained operatically but has been composing for and singing in an eclectic jazz/pop band for a few years. She's got a gorgeous voice that I find mesmerizing. Her songs tend to be sad and focused on the struggles of love (first album) and war (the second album). I probably saw almost ten of their live shows in NYC in 2006-2007. A very special group and as of yet unsigned and not well known. Also check out her Fairwell, Recruit video.
